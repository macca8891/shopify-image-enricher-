To use BuckyDrop rates at checkout:

  1. Go to Settings → Shipping & delivery
  2. Click "Manage rates" for your shipping zone
  3. Click "Add rate"
  4. Select "Use carrier or app to calculate rates"
  5. Choose "BuckyDrop Shipping" from the dropdown

How It Works

BuckyDrop Shipping integrates with Shopify's Carrier Calculated Shipping (CCS) to show real-time shipping rates at checkout.

  • Reads product dimensions and weight from metafields
  • Calls BuckyDrop API to calculate shipping rates
  • Shows shipping rates at checkout
  • Rates are calculated in real-time based on cart contents and destination
